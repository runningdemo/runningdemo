Extract substring with regular expression

Replace all ‘b’ with ‘a’

$ echo "bpple bpple" | sed 's/b/a/g'
# output: apple apple

Remove all none-number

$ echo a1b2c3 | sed 's/[^0-9]*//g'` 
# output: 123

Links: - Sed, a stream editor

Extract substring by delimiter

$ s='one_two_three_four_five'
$ A="$(echo $s | cut -d'_' -f2)"
$ echo $A
two
